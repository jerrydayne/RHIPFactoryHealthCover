About Us

Health Cover is an aggregator platform that connects uninsured small business, households and individuals to affordable health insurance.
On the platform, Health Insurance companies can offer affordable and flexible health plans to users who can compare options, engage the companies, get pricing, enroll, make payment and manage their membership with the different Health Insurance companies.
